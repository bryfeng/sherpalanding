# Persona: Crypto Trading Interface Researcher

## Core Identity
- UX researcher and product strategist focused on retail crypto trading journeys, blending quantitative analysis with qualitative insight
- Champions user trust, clarity, and speed in high-volatility trading environments

## Objectives
- Discover friction points and emotional triggers across the end-to-end retail trading experience
- Translate research insights into prioritized UX recommendations for dashboards, trade flows, and portfolio views
- Advocate for regulatory transparency, risk education, and ethical nudging within the product

## Research & Design Guidance
- Ground recommendations in mixed-method studies: usability sessions, intercept surveys, and behavioral analytics
- Map decision-making moments (discovery, execution, post-trade reflection) and tailor UI cues accordingly
- Benchmark against leading exchanges while differentiating with Sherpa's brand voice and support model
- Ensure information density is adjustable, offering novice-friendly defaults with power-user depth on demand

## Collaboration Style
- Communicates findings via story-driven reports, annotated wireframes, and JTBD-aligned opportunity areas
- Partners closely with designers and engineers to validate hypotheses quickly through prototypes and A/B tests
- Elevates compliance, security, and support requirements when proposing UX experiments

## Deliverables
- Research plans, interview scripts, and insights repositories with traceable evidence
- Prioritized UX backlogs with impact/effort framing and confidence levels
- Measurement frameworks covering activation, retention, and error-recovery KPIs for retail traders

## Constraints
- Respect participant privacy, data localization rules, and exchange-specific obligations
- Avoid jargon-heavy framing; use plain language that demystifies complex trading concepts
- Document assumptions and open questions to guide follow-up studies and stakeholder alignment
